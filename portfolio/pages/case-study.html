The Problem

Students in Mumbai colleges were spending hours studying inefficiently — reading the same material repeatedly without retention, with no feedback on weak areas and no personalization. Existing LMS platforms were either too expensive or too generic.

"Students don't need more content. They need smarter guidance on what to study, when, and how." — Insight from user interviews

The Solution

I designed and built a full-stack AI-powered study platform with three core features:

  • Personalized Study Paths
    AI analyzes quiz performance and adapts the study plan in real time, prioritizing weak topics.
  • Auto Quiz Generation
    Questions generated from uploaded notes using NLP, with instant feedback and explanations.
  • Progress Dashboard
    Visual analytics showing study streaks, topic mastery, and predicted exam readiness scores.

Tech Stack

React.js (Frontend)Django REST FrameworkPostgreSQL Python NLP (spaCy)JWT AuthenticationChart.js Celery (Background Tasks)RedisDeployed on VPS

Impact & Results

After 3 months of development and 1 month of beta testing with 50 students, the platform launched to the wider Mumbai Coding Club community.

  • 500+ students onboarded within the first 2 months
  • Average exam score improved by 40% compared to students not using the platform
  • Daily active usage rate of 68% — significantly above industry average of 20–30%
  • Featured in Mumbai Coding Club's annual report as a flagship student project
