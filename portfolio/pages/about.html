My Story

Faika Khan
Full Stack Developer

Hey! I'm Faika — a Computer Science student at the University of Mumbai who discovered a passion for web development and never looked back. What started as curiosity about "how websites work" turned into 2 years of intensive building, learning, and growing.

During my internship at Mumbai Coding Club (2024 — 2025), I went from writing my first HTML tags to architecting full-stack applications with AI integration. I've built 25+ projects spanning student management systems, LMS platforms, AI-powered study tools, and complete e-commerce solutions.

I believe great software is a blend of thoughtful engineering and human-centered design. When I'm not coding, I'm exploring AI tools, contributing to open-source, and mentoring junior developers at the Ummeed Foundation.
